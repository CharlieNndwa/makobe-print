Services We Provided

These are the services which our agency provides:

  • Digital Printing

    Makobe Print offers high-quality digital printing services for a variety of materials including manuals, booklets, magazines, and business cards. With state-of-the-art technology, we ensure crisp, vibrant results that make your brand stand out.

  • Large Format Printing

    Whether you need PVC banners, stickers, or other large format materials, Makobe Print delivers premium quality printing solutions. Our attention to detail ensures your message is conveyed boldly and effectively, capturing attention wherever it's displayed.

  • Lithography

    From calendars and posters to flyers and corporate folders, Makobe Print specializes in lithographic printing to meet all your marketing needs. Our printing techniques guarantee sharp imagery and professional presentation for your promotional materials.

  • Silk screening

    Elevate your brand with our expert silk screening services, perfect for customizing apparel like T-shirts, caps, and conference bags with precision and style. Makobe Print ensures durable, eye-catching designs that leave a lasting impression.

  • Embroidery

    Makobe Print offers expert embroidery services for golf shirts, corporate uniforms, and more. With meticulous attention to detail, we transform garments into professional, branded attire that enhances your corporate image.

  • Promotional Items

    Make a lasting impression with our range of promotional items, including branded pens, rulers, and mugs. Makobe Print ensures high-quality customization that reinforces your brand identity and leaves a lasting impression with clients and customers.
